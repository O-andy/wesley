THE SCHOOL

WELSEY HIGH SCHOOL was established on 4th january 1964 by the methodist mission then, but now called methodist church Nigeria. The school started with the population of thirthy six (36) students on welsey girls high school ground, now bishop okwoche memorial methodist high school otukpo as temporary site. The school moved finally to the present site in 1968. the school was elevated to the status of higher school certificate (HSC) awarding institution in 1973. The aim of methodist church Nigeria in establishing the school is to give holistic education to both boys and girls in and outside the immediate community. Today the school has contribute in numerous ways to the socio-political, educational, religious and economical development of this country.

FOUNDER

John Wesley was an English cleric, theologian, and evangelist, who was a leader of a revival movement within the Church of England known as Methodism. The societies he founded became the dominant form of the independent Methodist movement that continues to this day.
